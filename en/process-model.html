Demo note

  • Planning values show adjustable duration classes.
  • Critical path shows waiting dependencies.
  • Parallel work shows steps that can start at the same time.
  • XNP and card reader remain the professional workstation and signature context before filings.
  • XNotar and XJustiz remain regulated electronic handovers for register and land-register context.
  • Notariat8 shows structure and review up to those boundaries, does not replace those systems and shows no client data.

Duration and critical path

Planning values, not official averages

  • Internal reviewhours to days
  • External responsesweeks
  • Complex executionweeks to months
  • Can run in parallel: tax office, municipality, priority notice, financing and release documents can start after notarization.
  • Blocks the critical path: purchase-price maturity waits for the relevant approvals, responses and rank checks.
  • Planning values, not official averages: duration classes are editable parameters for matter planning.
